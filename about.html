About JustForVibes

The story behind the Feelings Calculator

Who We Are

JustForVibes is a collection of free, fun web tools built for one simple reason — to make you smile, spark a debate, or give you something hilarious to send to your group chat. We believe the internet needs more simple, shareable joy.

"We make tools that are 100% fun and 0% science. Take the numbers with a pinch of salt — and a lot of humor."

The Feelings Calculator

Our flagship tool — the Feelings Calculator — lets you calculate the love, crush, friendship, hate, obsession, and more between any two people. Type two names, pick an emotion, and get a percentage. Same names always give the same result — it's deterministic, which makes sharing results with friends even more fun.

You can even create a Custom emotion — type anything from "cringe" to "loyalty" and the calculator will do its thing. 😄
